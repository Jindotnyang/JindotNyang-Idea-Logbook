# JindotNyang-Idea-Logbook
Timestamped product strategy & monetization concepts for AI platforms.  
This repository exists to **publish ideas with public timestamps** (Git commit history) and to share practical frameworks derived from real-world incentive systems.

> Trust-first monetization.  
> Ads are not the product — **answers are**.

---

## Why this repo?
AI products face a structural challenge:
- inference cost grows with usage,
- but free-tier monetization is limited,
- and aggressive ads can destroy trust.

This logbook proposes systems that:
- **monetize without contaminating answers**
- convert free-tier traffic from cost center → revenue center
- use incentives (credits) to control compute cost
- increase retention via habit loops (daily check-ins)
